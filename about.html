ABOUT ME

Hello there! My name is Gabriel and I'm a concept artist and illustrator. I'm from Gothenburg, Sweden which is also where I'm currently at doing freelance work.

In upper secondary school I studied Digital Production learning software such as Maya, Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects. In 2017 I graduated from the University of Skövde with a Bachelors Degree in Media Arts, Aesthetics and Narration at the Video Game Development programme with focus on 2D graphics.

I've been doing freelance work since 2013 but properly founded my own company in 2016.
